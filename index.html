Discuss the project
What is 3D scanning ?

3D scanning is a non-contact technology that captures the shape of physical objects using a laser. It creates precise digital copies of any room or building.

How accurate are your measurements ?

We use professional Leica measuring instruments that provide accuracy within 1 mm. This significantly exceeds the accuracy of manual measurements with a tape measure.

What files does the client receive ?

The client receives measured drawings and digital models in .DWG, .DXF, and if necessary .PDF formats, as well as a detailed photo report of the site.

Who are your typical clients ?

We work primarily with interior designers, design engineers, and furniture manufacturers who require detailed measurements and scanning of spaces.

How fast is the process ?

Every project is unique. Deadlines are tailored individually based on complexity and scale. We value your time and guarantee a balance: speed, accuracy, and no compromises.

In which countries do you operate ?

We work all over Europe. Our team is fully mobile and ready to travel to your site in any European country with our professional equipment.
